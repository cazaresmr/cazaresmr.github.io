Polynomial Functions

Recall that a monomial is a number, a variable, or the product of a number and one or more variables with whole number exponents. A polynomial is a monomial or a sum of monomials. A polynomial function is a function of the form where , the exponents are all whole numbers, and the coefficients are all real numbers. For this function, an is the leading coefficient, n is the degree, and a0 is the constant term. A polynomial function is in standard form when its terms are written in descending order of exponents from left to right.
